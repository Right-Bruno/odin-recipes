Sugar Salad

Short Description of Sugar Salad

A refreshing and flavorful salad that's perfect for summer. This simple salad is made with a sweet and tangy dressing that complements the crunchy vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 Cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 Cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 Cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 Cup sugar
  • 1/4 Cup vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
