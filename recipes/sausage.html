Grilled Sausage

Short Description of Grilled Sausage

A classic barbecue staple that's quick and easy to prepare. Grilled sausage is a delicious and versatile dish that can be served as a main course or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Sausage (Your choice of flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
