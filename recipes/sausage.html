Grilled Sausage

Short Description of Grilled Sausage

A classic barbecue staple that's quick and easy to prepare. Grilled sausage is a delicious and versatile dish that can be served as a main course or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Sausage (Your choice of flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat oven to 400oC
  2. Scrub potatoes clean and pierce each potato several times with a fork.
  3. Rub potatoes with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place potatoes directly on oven rack and bake for 45-60 minutes, or until tender when pierced with a fork.
  5. Let cool slightly before cutting in half and serving.
