Crispy Baked Potatoes

Short description of Cripsy Baked Potatoes

A Classic side dish that's easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser. These crispy baked potatoes are perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 oC
  2. Wash and peel potatoes
  3. Cut potatoes into 1-inch cubes
  4. Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  5. Spread potatoes on a baking sheet in a single layer
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and tender.
  7. Serve hot
