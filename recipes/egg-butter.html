Egg Butter

Short Note on Egg Butter

Egg Butter is a rich and creamy spread perfect for toast or sandwiches. This simple recipe combines the richness of butter with the savory flavor of eggs, creating a delicious and versatile topping

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Butter

Instructions

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites.
  2. Whisk the egg yolks until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the melted butter, whisking continuously until combined.
  4. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 30 minutes to set.
  5. Serve on toast, sandwiches, or as a topping for other dishes.
